About Daniel Séguin, Organiste

Daniel has been played music at the organ since the early 80's. He has taken organ courses with a great teacher, Louis Cyr in Hawkesbury, Ontario. Daniel has accumulated hundreds of music sheets and still playing on his new Roland AT-100. He particular enjoyed his summer 1987 playing at the Tourne-Broche dinning room in Lachute, Quebec. When he is not playing the organ, he enjoys listening to his favorite musicians: André Gagnon, Alexandra Streliski, Richard Abel, and Frank Mills.
